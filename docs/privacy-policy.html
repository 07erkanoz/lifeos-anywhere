Privacy Policy

LifeOS AnyWhere — Last updated: February 19, 2026

Summary: LifeOS AnyWhere operates entirely on your local network. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All file transfers happen directly between your devices over Wi-Fi.

1. Introduction

LifeOS AnyWhere ("the App") is a free, open-source, cross-platform application for sharing files, clipboard content, and folders between devices on the same local network. This privacy policy explains how the App handles your data.

2. Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to remote servers. Specifically:

  • No analytics or tracking services are used.
  • No crash reporting services are used.
  • No advertising SDKs are included.
  • No user accounts or registration are required.
  • No data is sent to the developer or any third party.

3. Local Network Communication

The App communicates exclusively over your local Wi-Fi network to:

  • Discover devices: The App sends UDP multicast packets on your local network to find other devices running LifeOS AnyWhere.
  • Transfer files: Files are sent directly from one device to another over your local network using HTTP. No data passes through external servers.
  • Share clipboard: Clipboard text and images are transferred directly between devices on the local network.
  • Sync folders: Folder synchronization operates between devices on the same local network.

4. Permissions

The App requests the following permissions, all of which are used solely for local network functionality:

  • Internet & Wi-Fi State: Required for local network device discovery and file transfer.
  • Storage: Required to read files you choose to share and to save received files.
  • Location (Android): Required by Android to obtain Wi-Fi network name (SSID) for device discovery. The App does not track your geographic location.
  • Camera: Used only for scanning QR codes to quickly connect devices. No photos or videos are captured or stored.
  • Notifications: Used to show file transfer progress and completion status.
  • Nearby Wi-Fi Devices (Android 13+): Required by Android for local network peer discovery.

5. Data Storage

  • Received files are saved to the location you choose on your device.
  • Transfer history and clipboard history are stored locally on your device using SharedPreferences. This data never leaves your device.
  • App settings (theme, language, download path) are stored locally on your device.

6. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

7. Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The App is a general-purpose utility tool and is not directed at children.

8. Open Source

LifeOS AnyWhere is open source under the MIT License. You can review the complete source code at github.com/07erkanoz/lifeos-anywhere.

9. Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository or visit lifeos.com.tr.
